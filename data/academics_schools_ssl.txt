
School of Social Sciences and Languages
The School of Social Sciences and Languages (SSL) was founded in 2020, having previously operated as the Division of Social Sciences and Languages since VIT Chennai’s inception in 2010. Aligned with its vision and mission, the School is dedicated to delivering high-quality education and advanced research, aiming to make a global impact.
Vision Statement of the School of Social Sciences and Languages
To be a centre of academic excellence by fostering holistic growth and development in students
Mission Statement of the School of Social Sciences and Languages
To mould students from different cross-sections of society by enhancing professional competence, self – confidence and a positive attitude that would help in the economic development of the nation.
To impart linguistic skills through innovative teaching–learning processes.
To inspire and empower students from diverse backgrounds to become innovative leaders and entrepreneurs who will contribute to the betterment of society with ethical and social responsibility.
The School is structured into three key divisions: the Division of English, the Division of Foreign Languages, and the Division of Social Sciences. Our accomplished faculty members specialize in diverse fields, including English, Economics, French, German, Japanese, Political Science, Psychology, Spanish, Sociology, and Social Work. The School offers two programmes B.Sc (Hons) in Economics and M.Sc (Integrated) in Psychology. The SSL also offers a wide range of university core and elective courses to students across various programs at the Institute, including B.Tech., M.Tech., MCA, MBA, M.Sc., B.A., LL.B (Hons), BBA, LL.B (Hons), and B.Sc.
English
The English wing at VIT Chennai consists of 51 faculty members with a majority holding doctorates from institutes of repute. We as a team are committed to equipping our students, research scholars, staff and faculty members with the essential language, communication and soft skills, giving them a cutting edge and an added advantage over their peers and competitors. We work to be the wind beneath their wings that helps them soar above the rest in all walks of life.
Courses Offered: A variety of English courses are being offered for one, two or three semesters to all students registered under the various programmes in the Institute. The students have two or four hours of contact classes including Theory and/or Practical and/or Project components. An entry level proficiency test, called English Proficiency Test (EPT), is given to all the students and based on its results students are streamed into various batches and allotted to take appropriate course(s). Social Sciences
The Social Sciences wing at VIT Chennai comprises 21 faculty members, the majority of whom hold doctorates from esteemed institutions. Recognizing that engineering students must complement their technical expertise with an understanding of the society they inhabit, the division is dedicated to fostering awareness of social structures, culture, media, ethics, values, power, politics, finance, governance, and the complexities of national and international affairs. Students are encouraged to recognize psychological, social, economic, political, and ethical challenges they may encounter and are guided in exploring effective strategies to navigate them.
