Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) University offers a two-year Master of Business Administration (MBA) program designed to provide students with the skills and knowledge required to become successful business leaders. Here is some information about the MBA program offered by VIT University:
Curriculum: The MBA program at VIT University covers various management subjects, including accounting, economics, marketing, operations, and human resources. The curriculum is designed to develop students’ analytical, critical thinking, and decision-making skills through a combination of classroom lectures, case studies, group discussions, and projects. Faculty: The MBA program at VIT University has a team of experienced faculty members who have extensive industry and academic experience. They use their expertise to guide students and provide them with insights into real-world business scenarios. Industry Exposure: VIT University’s MBA program provides students with ample opportunities to interact with industry professionals through guest lectures, industry visits, and internships. This exposure helps students to understand the current business trends, challenges, and opportunities.
