Gymnasium
The Institute newest facilities for students include two gymnasia, one exclusively for men, and one exclusively for women. A central multi-facility piece of equipment enables several enthusiasts to work out at the same time.
Women students have a 4-station multi-gym of their own. Both gyms are spacious and well equipped with modern facilities.
GYMNASIUM Timings are as follows:
Monday to Friday
Saturday & Sunday
Note: Sports costume is compulsory during the practice session. ie. [T-shirts, shorts/Tracksuits with canvas]
